A visual-inertial SLAM system extended to a **multi-camera** configuration for
wider field-of-view coverage and improved robustness under degenerate motion and
low-texture scenes. The work covers extrinsic calibration across cameras, tightly
coupled fusion with inertial measurements, and evaluation on real sequences.

This work was accepted at **IntRob 2026**.
